# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Video engineering and devops teams who manage high-performance video ingestion, encoding, storage optimization, and secure distribution. They need a single-pane-of-glass telemetry console that is clean, responsive, and robust under pressure.

## Product Purpose
Provide high-performance video ingestion telemetry, real-time transcoding metrics, compression efficiency diagnostics, and time-sensitive secure playback link building.

## Brand Personality
Precise, engineering-focused, robust, modern (amber accents, dark theme).

## Anti-references
- Overly generic SaaS templates with bland gray/white layouts.
- Unnecessary animations that distract from monitoring telemetry.
- Side-stripe borders, text/metric overflow, or hidden clipping regions.

## Design Principles
- Telemetry First: Real-time graphs and parameters are clearly visible with readable typography.
- Clear Visual States: Ingestion steps (Ingest, Analyze, Transcode, Optimize, Deliver) have distinct, active states with laser-scan telemetry indicators, not static stuck states.
- High Contrast and Focus: Use proper contrast and bright amber active indications to guide users to primary controls.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG Level AA compliance. High contrast text, proper focus indicators, and reduced-motion styling variables where layout transforms are used.
